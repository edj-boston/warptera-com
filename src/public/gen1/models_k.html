147 name: Keystone
genre: walker/motored
comments: This is a simple implementation of my Linear_Wedge_Motor using short tension springs and leg extensions. The word keystone is the name for a uniquely shaped block that fits into the top of a stone archway. I thought my motor was vaguely reminiscent of that shape.
293 name: Kiloton
genre: walker/motored
comments: Kiloton has the same linear motor combination with RAM modifications as Spheric. It walks okay but has the same general problem as Muse does in that the stride is a little awkward. I think smaller motors or more space between them would've made it better.
320 name: King_Crab
genre: walker/motored
comments: King_Crab uses 2pt Phased Compound Motors to drive a counter rotor linkage in the bottom. Then I hit upon the idea of using 4pt anchored linear motors to brace the tops of the 2pt ones, then I used the anchor points to mount the legs onto.
